Welcome to Kolappuram

Nestled in the heart of Kerala's Malappuram district, Kolappuram offers a unique blend of traditional charm and modern luxury. Our carefully curated experiences bring you the best of this serene village.

Our Story

Kolappuram is a peaceful village in Malappuram district, Kerala, known for its lush landscapes and rich cultural heritage. While little is documented about its specific history, it shares in Kerala's storied past and the legacy of the Malabar region.

Today, we've carefully selected the finest accommodations and experiences in Kolappuram to bring you luxury amidst authentic village life. Our philosophy combines impeccable service with the natural beauty of this special place.
